You have rented some movies for your kids:

  • The little mermaid (for 3 days),
  • Brother Bear (for 5 days, they love it),
  • Hercules (1 day, you don't know yet if they're going to like it)

If price for a movie per day is $3, how much will you have to pay?

var lm = 3
var bb = 5
var h = 1
var cost = 3
total = cost * (lm + bb + h)
return total total = 27

Suppose you're working as a contractor for 3 companies:
Google, Amazon and Facebook, they pay you a different rate per hour.

  • Google pays $400,
  • Amazon $380,
  • Facebook $350.
How much will you receive in payment for this week? You worked:
  • 10 hours for Facebook,
  • 6 hours for Google
  • 4 hours for Amazon.

var FbHours = 10
var GHours = 6
var AHours = 4
var FbCost = FbHours * 350
var GCost = Ghours * 400
var ACost = AHours * 380
var total = FbCost + GCost + ACost
return total
total = 7420
